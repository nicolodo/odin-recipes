Sausage and Mash

ingredients

  • sausage
  • potatoes
  • butter
  • milk
  • seasonings
  • onions
  • broth
  • wine

recipe

  1. Cook the sausages
  2. Boil, drain,mash and season the potatoes
  3. Cook the onions in butter, then add the wine broth, reduce
  4. serve the sausages with the potatoes and pour the gravy on top
