Lasagna recipe

Description

This is a delicious lasagna the likes of which has never been seen before

MMMMMMMMMmmmmm wow!

follow along and you too will have a lasagna

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • mincemeat
  • lasagna sheets
  • Salt
  • pepper
  • parsley
  • basil

Steps

  1. Get the ingredients
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned
  3. Stir in tomatoes, water, parsley, basil, salt, pepper
  4. cook the lasagna noodles in boiling water for 9 minutes
  5. mix ricotta with egg, parsley and salt
  6. preheat the oven to 190C
  7. spread meat sauce in baking dish, cover with lasagna sheets
  8. repeat previous step until out of ingredients
  9. cover with foil
  10. bake for 25m remove foil and bake for another 25m
  11. rest for 15m to cool before serving
