lasagna

ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • cheese
  • mincemeat
  • pasta casing

recipe

  1. put pasta casing in dish
  2. put tomatoes and mincemeat on top
  3. add layer of pasta
  4. repeat until dish is full
  5. when full put final layer of pasta
  6. add cheese
  7. put in oven until burnt
  8. remove from oven
  9. order lasagna
